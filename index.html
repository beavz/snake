SNAKE

Get points by eating the food, but don't run into your friend, the wall, or yourself!

SPACE will start the game, then pause or unpause both snakes.

ENTER will pause or unpause only the green snake, allowing you to play alone should you so choose.

UP DOWN RIGHT LEFT will control the blue snake.

W S D A will control the green snake.

  • GREEN: 0
  • Press SPACE to begin.
  • BLUE: 0
